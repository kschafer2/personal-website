Education

My education in Lee's Summit, Missouri started at Hazel Grove Elementary, took me to Bernard C. Campbell Middle School, and ended at Lee's Summit North High School. I then traveled to the desert of Nevada and furthered my education at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas for one year. I returned closer to home and graduated from the University of Missouri, Columbia with degrees in biology and psychology. Now, I am finishing my degree in computer science at Columbia College of Missouri and will have graduated at the end of April, 2019.

The University of Nevada, Las Vegas was founded in 1957. The university has one of the top hospitality programs in the country and has the only law and dental schools in the state of Nevada.

The University of Missouri, Columbia, colloquially Mizzou, was founded in 1839. Mizzou is an important research university with the most powerful university research reactor in the world. The university's journalism school is one of the oldest journalism schools in the world, and is still well-renowned.

Columbia College was founded in Columbia, Missouri in 1851 as a private, non-profit college. There are now 40 campuses across the United States offering 18 online degree programs.
